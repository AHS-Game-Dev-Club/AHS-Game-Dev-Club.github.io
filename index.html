Welcome To Our Site

AHS Game Development Club is a sub-club of Computer Science First at American High School dedicated to exploring the world of game development and programming. We meet every week to teach the Unity game engine and C#, creating several games throughout the year.

Along with this, we delve into game architecture, 2D graphics, optimization techniques, and creating game art. We may also host game jams or collaborate with other clubs. We welcome all, regardless of programming or game development experience.
